Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for begginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in yoga, this
60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana(proper alignment and posture),
pranayama(breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed to intermediate students,beginners are welcome to sample this
60 minute class of poses that focuses on breath-synchronized movement -you will inhale and exhale as
you flow energetically through yoga poses.
Restoration Yoga
This 90 minute class features very slow movement and long poses that a supported by a chair
or wall.This calming, Restoration experience is suitable for students of any level of experience.
This practice can be a perfect way to rehabilitate an injury.

Relax anytime with savasana

Prepare yourself for savasana .Lie down your yoga mat with your arms on the side and your palms
up.Close your eyes and breathe slowly and deeply. Sink into the mat and let your worries slip away.
When you're ready,When you're ready,roll on your side and use your arm to push yourself on the sitting
position with crossed legs.Place your hands in a prayer position. Be grateful for all that your have in
life.Namaste.
